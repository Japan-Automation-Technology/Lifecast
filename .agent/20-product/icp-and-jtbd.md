# ICP and JTBD

## Creator ICP
- toC creative builders where process itself is audience-worthy.
- Examples: indie game creators, small hardware product teams.
- Needs:
  - Show progress continuously.
  - Convert audience interest to pre-order-like support.
  - Incorporate supporter feedback visibly.

## Supporter ICP
- Users who already consume creator progress content on short-video apps.
- Motivated by cheering, belonging, and visible impact.
- Needs:
  - Discover projects quickly.
  - Understand support plans quickly.
  - Get recognition as supporter and see project outcomes.

## Core jobs
- Creator job: "Help me turn process content into funding while building trust."
- Supporter job: "Help me support and influence creators I care about with low friction."
