# KPI Definitions

Primary ranking objective:
- maximize `support_conversion_rate x average_support_amount x repeat_support_rate`

Metric definitions:
- `support_conversion_rate`: unique users with `payment_succeeded` divided by eligible users exposed to support opportunity.
- `average_support_amount`: average successful payment amount.
- `repeat_support_rate`: share of supporters with more than one successful support in defined window.

Secondary product health indicators:
- Video-to-support click-through rate.
- Checkout completion rate after support click.
- Comments per active viewer rate.
- Supports per user.
- Active time per user.

Measurement note:
- Secondary indicators support diagnosis but do not replace primary conversion definition.
