# Trust and Safety Policy

Operating posture:
- Strict, explicit, and evidence-based decisions.
- Preserve logs for disputes and post-incident review.

## Pre-publication minimum checks
- Identity reality check (KYC baseline).
- Physical return/reward disclosure.
- Delivery schedule disclosure.
- Prototype presence disclosure.
- Copyright infringement screening.
- Financial-instrument-like characteristics screening.

## Stop conditions
- False or deceptive project statements.
- Copyright violations.
- Anti-social affiliation or equivalent prohibited status.
- Material progress non-reporting.
- Sufficient volume of high-trust reports.

## Appeal flow
- Stop notice is issued.
- Creator has 72 hours to submit explanation.
- Operations team makes final decision based on policy and evidence.

## Logging requirements
- Preserve moderation actions, evidence snapshots, and decision rationale.
- Keep event and policy logs queryable for dispute handling.
