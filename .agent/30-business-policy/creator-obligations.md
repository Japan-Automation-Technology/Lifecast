# Creator Obligations

Required disclosures and behavior:
- Maintain accurate project claims.
- Disclose prototype status.
- Provide realistic delivery schedule.
- Define refund conditions per plan.
- Notify delays and schedule changes promptly.

Trust-related obligations:
- Respond to major supporter concerns in a timely manner.
- Maintain progress communication to reduce uncertainty and disputes.

Enforcement:
- Violations may trigger project stop, appeal workflow, and account-level actions.
